VolatileAmple (VAMP)

The first tokens which directly responds to Technical indicator to increase volatility. Sister Token of StableAmple (SAMP).

The first project to dynamically update supply using TA to increase volatility of the coin.

If the coin is overbought...

VAMP will increase the total supply in order to encourage selling.

If the coin is oversold...

VAMP will decrease the total supply in order to encourage buying.

Extreme volatility for active traders

Providing high volatility will reward users who trade frequently.



VAMP uses RSI indicators to determine rebases.

Every day, VAMP's oracle checks the hourly chart from the last 24 hrs and counts the number of times the price went into "overbought" and "oversold" territory. It then gets the difference between those two indicators, and adjusts the supply depending on the number of hourly closes in the dominant territory multiplied by 0.5%. So, in the above image, VAMP would decrease its supply by 4% at rebase.
